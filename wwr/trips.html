Whitewater Thrill: The River Surge Adventure

Brace yourself for an adrenaline-charged journey through Class III and IV rapids on a stunning mountain river.
This full-day rafting trip takes you through roaring whitewater, narrow canyons, and pristine forest views.
Ideal for adventurous beginners and experienced paddlers alike.
  • Duration: 1 day (6–7 hours on the water)
  • Includes:
    1. Guide
    2. Lunch
    3. safety gear
    4. shuttle transport
  • Cost: $120 per person

Scenic Drift: The Lazy River Escape

A calm and refreshing rafting experience ideal for families, couples, or anyone looking to relax and enjoy nature at a gentle pace
This float trip winds through a wide, slow-moving section of the river, offering crystal-clear waters and lush riverside views.
Safe for children 4 years and up. Life jackets provided for all.
  • Professional river guide
  • Life jackets and paddles
  • Light refreshments (bottled water, juice, fruit snacks)
  • Safety briefing and shuttle service back to launch point
  • Costs:
    1. Eid $60 per person
    2. Children under 10: $40
    3. Group discount (6+): 10% off total price

The Ultimate Canyon Expedition

A 3-day rafting and camping adventure through scenic canyons and thrilling Class III–IV rapids.
Includes riverside camping, guided hikes, gourmet meals, and all essential gear.
Perfect for outdoor enthusiasts seeking a rugged, unforgettable wilderness escape.
  • Experienced guide team and safety crew
  • All meals (including vegetarian/vegan options)
  • Dry bags for personal items
  • Tents, sleeping bags, mats, and camp chairs
  • Shuttle transportation to/from the river
  • Emergency satellite communication
  • Costs
    1. $450 per person
    2. Optional gear rental (wet suits/dry jackets): $25 extra
    3. Early bird discount (book 30+ days in advance): $50 off
